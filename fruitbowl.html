Fruitbowl Hutong
What?
The fruit bowl was laser-cut from wood and gets additional support from the fine wire frames, which further emphasise the rounded shape of the wood.
Method?
Since an appropriately thick material had to be used for the production in order to guarantee sufficient stability, the wood was bent over steam after it had been lase- red, thus retaining the desired shape, which can be easily stretched between the frames, thus avoiding additional glue.
