Bachelor Thesis
Focus of the bachelor thesis
For my bachelor thesis I create a furniture configurator that presents the product and its advantages. The shelf will be flexible to assemble and disassemble and will be made of sustainable and CO2 neutral materials. This should be communicated to the user on the website without putting too much focus on the eco image. It is intended to appeal to young families who want to settle in for a longer period of time, but want to remain flexible. In order to give the project a suitable framework, a CI will also be designed.
