Billie Jean
What?
It is a floor lamp made by hand from plaster and bandages.
Method?
As a bedside lamp or reading lamp it crea- tes a very warm and pleasant atmosphere due to the uneven light & shadow play. The bulb can be replaced by lifting one of the wings, which is attached to the upper end by means of a magnet (fig. far right). Even during the day, it impresses with its black / white design, which has a sculptural appearance.
design by Elena Burgmair
