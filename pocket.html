Pocketlight
What?
The task was to design a pocketlight that is small and practical. The first prototype was made with the 3D printer.
Method?
The hook shape makes it possible to attach the lamp to flaps, for example on the waist-band of trousers. At the same time, the hol-der lies comfortably in the hand when the lamp is used in the usual way. The housing offers enough space for two AA batteries and two LED lights.
